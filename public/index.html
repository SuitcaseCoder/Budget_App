BudgIt

A visual budget-space for your upcoming events!

    Here's how:

  1. Add your upcoming events to the list below
  2. Now that your future's laid out, click on an event to 'BudgIt' its expenses
  3. Where do you want the money to go? Add it to your expenses
  4. Visualize your spending by adjusting your 'BudgIt'
  5. Can you stay UNDER 'BudgIt'?
